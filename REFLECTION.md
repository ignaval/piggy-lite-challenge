# Reflection

_½–1 page. This is part of what we evaluate — be candid; an honest "it depends,
because…" beats a tidy answer._

## How did you use AI tools?

What did you delegate to AI vs. write or decide yourself? Which tools, and for
what (scaffolding, tests, debugging, naming, docs)?

## Where did you override or correct the AI?

A concrete example where an AI suggestion was wrong, risky, or off-convention —
what you did instead, and why.

## The hardest open decision

Which of the spec's open questions (period semantics & reset; block vs. partial
vs. warn vs. require-approval; whether funding affects usage; where enforcement
lives; which error wins) was hardest? What did you choose, what did you reject,
and what would change your mind?

## What you cut, and what's next

What you deliberately left out for time, and the first things you'd do with
another day.
